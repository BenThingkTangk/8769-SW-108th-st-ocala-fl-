The Snowbird Intercept

Contact Oak Run clubhouse management and request to put a flyer on every golf cart for the open house Saturday. 300+ people use the golf course weekly.

#3

The Home Warranty Offer

Add a 1-year home warranty ($500 cost) to the listing. Eliminates the #1 buyer objection (fear of old systems) and makes your listing stand out in every comparison.

#4

The Rate Lock Guarantee

Partner with a local lender to offer a rate lock for qualified buyers. Post "Rate locked at 6.11% — apply now" in the listing. Creates urgency and differentiates.

The Virtual Open House

Host a 30-min live Zoom walkthrough for out-of-state 55+ buyers. Post in retirement Facebook groups nationwide. Record it and upload to YouTube.

The Lifestyle Video

90-second video: morning coffee on the lanai, golf cart to the clubhouse, pickleball, sunset. Not a house tour — a lifestyle sale. Target 55-75 in retirement FB groups.

#7

The Agent Incentive Bomb

Email every buyer's agent in Marion County: offer $1,500 buyer's agent bonus for any contract by April 1. Costs $1,500 but can generate 5-10 extra showings instantly.

#8
